California Golden Trout (Oncorhynchus mykiss aguabonita)

Native to California, the Golden Trout is celebrated for its vibrant coloration and is a prized species among anglers.

Brown Trout (Salmo trutta)

A widely distributed species, the Brown trout is known for its adaptability to various environments and is a favorite among anglers.

Bull Trout (Salvelinus confluentus)

Known for their size and preference for cold, clear waters, Bull trout are a species of char native to North American rivers and lakes.

Brook Trout (Salvelinus fontinalis)

Characterized by their distinctive marbled pattern and vibrant colors, Brook trout are native to Eastern North America and a favorite among anglers.

Lahontan Cutthroat Trout (Oncorhynchus clarki henshawi)

The Lahontan cutthroat trout, known for its large size and unique coloration, is native to the Lahontan basin of northern Nevada, eastern California, and southern Oregon.

Dolly Varden (Salvelinus malma)

A species of char native to cold-water tributaries of the Pacific Ocean in North America and Asia, the Dolly Varden is admired for its beautiful coloration and sporting quality.

Rainbow Trout (Oncorhynchus mykiss)

Renowned for their vibrant colors and acrobatic fighting when hooked, Rainbow trout are a popular species among anglers worldwide.
