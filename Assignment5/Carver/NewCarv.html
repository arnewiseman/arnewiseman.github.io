Carver Gym - Today

Today, Carver Gymnasium has been transformed into a state-of-the-art athletic complex following extensive renovations. The facility now includes modernized gym spaces, an upgraded arena for basketball and volleyball games, a comprehensive fitness center, and advanced training facilities for Western Washington University athletes. It remains a vibrant center for student life, promoting wellness and community engagement among students and staff alike.
