Old Main - Today

Over the years, Old Main has been meticulously preserved and updated to blend its historical charm with modern functionalities. Today, it continues to serve as the administrative heart of the university, housing the offices of the President and other key administrative personnel. The building also features updated classrooms equipped with the latest educational technology, facilitating a bridge between its rich past and its dynamic present. As the oldest building on campus, Old Main remains a symbol of the university's long-standing commitment to higher education and community involvement.
